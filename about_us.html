OpsySports International

We are a global, full-service management group providing services to professional football players. Our clients are our top priority. OPSYSPORTS is very selective during its client acquisition process, which allows us to provide each client with unmatched personal attention, guidance and services. Our goal is to provide the highest quality representation and management to athletes who want to maximize their potential on and off the pitch.

Partners and professional reach

OPSYSPORTS International has a very strong international football network through the development of personal and close relationships with club owners, sports directors, scouts and coaches, management and teams in Europe, America, Africa and Asia.
